    Kids Return

    Kids Return #

    Plot and review. #

    Movie focuses on the lives of two slacker best of friends, Masaru (Ken Kaneko) and Shinji (Masanobu Ando). Both live meaningless school bully lifestyle. Due to turn of events one (Shinji) starts his boxing career, the other one goes way of Yakuza. Movie’s straight forward narrative is easy to follow.

    Verdict. #

    Good movie.

    Interesting fact. #

    Ken Kaneko actually became professional fighter. And even fought Charles Bennett (Krazy Horse) at one point.
